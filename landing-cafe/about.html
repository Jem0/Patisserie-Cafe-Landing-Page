Discover Our Story

01

Charles Gardinier, our founder, is a self-taught baker who gave up everything to pursue after his passion for bread, pastries, and art. Paragon is the product of his creative expression and passions.


We will bring you the taste of France.

02

Paragon Patisserie dates its origin to 1986 and was born from the desire to recreate the taste of French pastries for the finest palette.

We deliver the finest in sugar-free and gluten-free products that are made from the highest-quality ingredients. We are commited to providing healthy, delectable products without compromising quality.

Consistent Excellence

Each and every product is hand crafted with intention; it is made to be healthy, delicious, and visually pleasing.

Gardinier's original creations are set to exceed expectations. The constant innovation and refinement are to ensure that excellence is met. The pursuit after excellence is why the patisserie and bakery is named Paragon.
